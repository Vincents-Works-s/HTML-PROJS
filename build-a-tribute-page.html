Ip Man

Wing Chun Grandmaster

Timeline of Ip Man's Life

  • 1893ㅤBorn and grew up in wealthy family in Foshan, Guangdong
  • 1904ㅤStarted learning Wing Chun from Chan Wah-shun at a young age
  • 1909ㅤIp, 16, challenged Wing Chun martial artist Leung Bik to a duel and was soundly beaten
  • 1909ㅤBegan training with Leung Bik
  • 1916ㅤMarried Cheung Wing-sing and had several children
  • 1919ㅤBecame police officer for Nationalist Government and taught Wing Chun to friends and subordinates
  • 1949ㅤAfter the Second Sino-Japanese War and the Chinese Civil War, Ip and his family left Foshan for Hong Kong
  • 1951ㅤDue to China and Hong Kong border conflict, Ip and his wife Cheung separated for good
  • 1953ㅤIp started his Wing Chun school in Hong Kong to support financial difficulties and his opium addiction. He slowly built fame through it
  • 1972ㅤDied at 79 from Laryngeal cancer

Ip Man is best known for spreading Wing Chun to the rest of the world through mentoring so many remarkable students. He was honored by his humble, persistent, and inspirational traits.
